  1. Go to strava.com/settings/api
  2. Click "Create App"
  3. Fill in the form:
    • Application Name: RUNNMATE Integration
    • Category: Training
    • Website: http://localhost:3000 (for development)
    • Authorization Callback Domain: localhost (for development)
